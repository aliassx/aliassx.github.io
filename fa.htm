How to use this tool?

Just click on the three little dots, and press (Latin) letters on your keyboard. They will be automatically changed into their Persian equivalent!

To add a dot above a letter, just press the Apostrophe. And for three dots, press the apostrophe two times.


Example: I want to type le letter خ. For this, I will type H (for ح) then the apostrophe.
